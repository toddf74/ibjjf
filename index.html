Training Plan: March 3 → April 17, 2026

This plan is designed to prepare you for the IBJJF Chicago Open (April 18–19, 2026). The goal is to arrive healthy, conditioned, and technically sharp. To do that, this guide coordinates your weekly Ladder strength training (5x/week), jiu jitsu classes (Mon/Wed + optional Sat), daily shoulder and knee rehab work, and progressive conditioning sessions. The structure balances performance improvement with pain reduction so you peak at the right time.

Non-Negotiable Rules

Pain rules Consistency No failure reps
  • Shoulder: pressing pain stays ≤ 3/10. If it hits 4+ → reduce ROM and/or load immediately.
  • Knee: pain stays ≤ 3/10 during leg work; next morning should be same or better than baseline.
  • Walking: minimum 30 minutes daily, done during the day (not after jiu jitsu).
  • No grinding / no failure on pressing or pull-ups while shoulder is reactive.
  • Protein target 180–200g/day.

Shoulder Routine (Daily, 12–15 min)

Do once daily (before lifting or before jiu jitsu). Goal: reduce pain and improve scap control.

  • Thoracic extension (foam roller) — 2 min
  • Dead hang (partial weight ok) — 2 x 30 sec
  • Serratus wall slides — 3 x 12
  • Scap push-ups — 3 x 12
  • Prone Y raise (light) — 3 x 12
  • Face pulls — 3 x 15
  • External rotation isometric (elbow at side) — 3 x 30 sec (pain ≤3/10)

Knee Routine (Tue/Thu mandatory, 8–10 min)

Optional extra on Saturday if the knee feels unstable.

  • Lateral step-down — 3 x 10 / side (3 sec down)
  • Copenhagen plank (short lever) — 3 x 30 sec / side
  • Split squat ISO hold (mid-range) — 2 x 30 sec / side

Pressing Substitutions (Locked)

Keep chest stimulus while removing high-irritation positions.

  • Allowed: neutral-grip DB press, floor press, landmine press, incline push-up (pain-free range)
  • Avoid for 3–4 weeks: dips, barbell bench to chest, incline bench, deep fly stretch, heavy overhead press, lateral raises (swap to scaption)
  • Pulling volume should be ≥ pressing volume.

Use effort targets: RPE 7 = ~3 reps in reserve; RPE 8 = ~2 reps in reserve.

Conditioning Overview

  • Friday Zone 2: 30–45 min incline walk pad (nose-breathable, full sentences).
  • Thursday intervals: progressive intervals; last hard interval session on Apr 10.
  • Jiu Jitsu: Mon/Wed (+ optional Sat open mat).

Phase Progression

  • Phase 1 (Mar 3–Mar 16): pain down + base built
  • Phase 2 (Mar 17–Apr 6): build match engine
  • Phase 3 (Apr 7–Apr 17): taper; arrive fresh
